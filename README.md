# Chocolate-Sales-Analysis
Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a chocolate sales dataset to uncover sales trends, product performance, and profitability insights.

The objective is to analyze revenue patterns, identify top-performing products, and generate business-driven recommendations to improve overall sales performance.


Business Objective:-

The primary goals of this analysis are:

Identify top-selling chocolate products

Analyze region-wise sales performance

Evaluate monthly and yearly revenue trends

Understand profit distribution

Generate actionable business recommendations


Tools & Technologies Used:-

Python

Pandas

NumPy

Matplotlib

Seaborn

Jupyter Notebook


Dataset Description:-

The dataset includes:

Product Name

Sales Amount

Profit

Region

Order Date

Quantity Sold

Category

Key Analysis Performed:-

Data Cleaning & Missing Value Treatment

Sales Trend Analysis (Monthly/Yearly)

Region-wise Revenue Comparison

Product-wise Performance Analysis

Profit Margin Evaluation

Top & Bottom Performing Products


Key Insights:-

Certain premium chocolate categories generate higher profit margins.

Sales peak during festive seasons.

A few regions contribute to the majority of total revenue.

Some high-sales products have low profit margins.


Business Recommendations:-

Focus marketing efforts on high-margin products.

Optimize pricing strategy for low-margin, high-volume items.

Expand distribution in high-performing regions.

Launch promotional campaigns during peak seasons.

Discontinue or reposition underperforming products.


Conclusion:-

This project demonstrates how sales data analysis can support strategic business decisions by identifying growth opportunities and profitability drivers.
